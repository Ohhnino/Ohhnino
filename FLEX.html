    "My Father Ati Lagno"

    Reflecting on my father, who is no longer with us, I find myself flexing the enduring strength of his memory. While he may no longer physically be present, the lessons he imparted, the values he instilled, and the love he shared are the weights I lift in the gym of life. It's not about showcasing physical prowess but about embodying the resilience and wisdom he passed on. In moments of challenge, I draw upon the mental and emotional fortitude he exemplified, flexing the muscles of determination and perseverance that he cultivated within me. In these quiet moments of reflection, I find a unique strength, an invisible flex that connects me to his enduring legacy, reminding me that his influence lives on in the way I navigate life's hurdles and triumphs.
